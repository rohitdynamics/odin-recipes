MEATBALLS RECIPE

Description

Italian-style meatballs are tender, juicy, and deeply flavorful thanks to a blend of meat, breadcrumbs, aromatics, and cheese. When cooked properly, the inside stays moist and soft while the exterior gets a nice golden crust. They pair wonderfully with pasta, serve well as appetizers, or fit perfectly into sandwiches.

Their charm comes from simple ingredients that transform into a satisfying dish. Mixed with herbs, parmesan, and onions, the meat develops a savory, comforting taste. Whether simmered in tomato sauce or served on their own, meatballs remain a beloved classic across cuisines.

Steps to cook :

  • Mix breadcrumbs with a little milk to soften.
  • Combine meat, egg, garlic, onion, parmesan, salt, pepper, and herbs.
  • Add softened breadcrumbs and mix gently.
  • Shape into equal-sized balls.
  • Pan-fry until browned on all sides.
  • Optional: simmer in tomato sauce for 15 minutes.
  • Serve hot.
