PASTA RECIPE

Description

This classic tomato-basil pasta is bright, aromatic, and incredibly simple. Fresh tomatoes and garlic simmer into a naturally sweet and tangy sauce that coats each strand of pasta beautifully. The fragrance of basil adds a fresh touch that elevates the whole dish.

Its charm comes from its minimal ingredients and quick cooking time, making it an ideal weeknight meal. The balance of acidity, herbs, and olive oil creates a comforting yet refreshing flavor that suits both beginners and experienced cooks.

Steps to cook :

  • Cook pasta in salted water until al dente.
  • Saute garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  • Add tomatoes, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Toss cooked pasta into the sauce.
  • Add fresh basil and mix.
  • Garnish with parmesan, if desired.
