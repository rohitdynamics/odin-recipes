PIZZA RECIPE

Description

Margherita pizza is the definition of Italian simplicity: a thin, crisp crust topped with tomato, mozzarella, and basil. Each element is fresh and minimal, creating a clean flavor profile that highlights quality ingredients. When baked, the cheese melts into creamy pools and the basil adds a herbaceous aroma.

Loved worldwide for its elegance and balance, this pizza focuses on pure and uncomplicated taste. Without heavy toppings, the dough and sauce shine through, creating a light yet satisfying meal thatâ€™s perfect for any occasion.

Steps to cook :

  • Stretch the pizza dough into a thin base.
  • Spread a light layer of tomato sauce.
  • Add slices of fresh mozzarella.
  • Bake at 220-250 deg C for 8-12 minutes.
  • Add fresh basil and drizzle olive oil after baking.
  • Slice and serve hot.
