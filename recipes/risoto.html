RISOTO RECIPE

Description

Mushroom risoto is creamy, comforting, and rich with earthy flavors. The arborio rice slowly absorbs warm broth, creating a velvety texture while still keeping a slight bite. Sautéed mushrooms add depth and make the dish hearty and satisfying.

Although risoto requires patience, the result is worth it—a luxurious, restaurant-style dish made with simple ingredients. The combination of butter, parmesan, and mushrooms produces a deep, savory flavor that works well as both a main and a side dish.

Steps to cook :

  • Warm broth in a separate pot.
  • Saute onions and garlic in butter or oil.
  • Add mushrooms and cook until soft.
  • Add rice and toast for 1-2 minutes.
  • Add a splash of white wine (optional) and let it evaporate.
  • Add hot broth one ladle at a time, stirring continuously.
  • Repeat until rice is creamy and cooked.
  • Stir in parmesan and adjust seasoning before serving.
