LASAGNA RECIPE

Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian baked dish known for its comforting richness and deep savory flavor. Each layer blends tender pasta sheets with creamy cheese and a hearty meat sauce, resulting in a warm, satisfying meal perfect for gatherings and weekend dinners. Its golden, bubbling top is one of the most iconic sights in comfort cooking.

What makes lasagna special is its balance-soft pasta, flavorful meat, tangy tomato sauce, and melty cheese combine into a single cohesive bite. The dish has evolved through countless family variations, but the essence remains the same: layers of simple ingredients transformed into something indulgent and timeless.

Steps to cook :

  • Saute onions and garlic in olive oil.
  • Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  • Add tomato sauce, herbs, salt, and pepper; simmer 15 minutes.
  • Layer baking dish: sauce / pasta sheets / ricotta / mozzarella / repeat.
  • End with cheese on top.
  • Bake at 180 deg C for 35-40 minutes until golden.
  • Rest for 10 minutes before cutting.
